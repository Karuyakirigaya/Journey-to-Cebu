    The Early History

    Cebu’s history began with the arrival of the Spanish explorers led by Ferdinand Magellan in 1521. Cebu was the first Spanish settlement in the Philippines and played a significant role in the early colonial history of the country.

    Colonial Era

    During the colonial period, Cebu continued to grow as a vital hub for trade and missionary work. The construction of significant structures such as Magellan’s Cross and the Basilica del Santo Niño marks this era.
